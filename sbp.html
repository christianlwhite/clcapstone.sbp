Mall Walkers


Autumn, Crystal and Queenie, three Louisville women in their mid 20s and fresh out of prison, find themselves immersed in the lives of a group of mallwalking, pickleballing senior citizens as they navigate whether to walk the straight and narrow or fall back into their old criminal ways.

Written, produced and directed by Highlands natives, this independently funded, hilarious and heartfelt comedy centered around the Mid-City Mall shows the true grit and beauty of life in Louisville. This project will not only help the budding Kentucky film industry, but it will also employ dozens of Kentuckians through production and will bring much needed attention to local businesses.

Why Kentucky

Kentucky has positioned itself to become the next epicenter of film making in the United States. In 2022, Kentucky passed an incentive program offering tax credits on film production in Kentucky in the range of 30-35%. This financial incentive has sparked a renewed interest in Kentucky and sparked ancillary industries, such as the the production soundstage in Louisville slated to open in 2026, to organically appear. Financial incentives and production capabilities aside, Kentucky offers a truly diverse selection of film shoot locations. The beauty and mystery of the mountains in the east, the rolling hills and farmland in the west, the bluegrass-covered savanahs in the central region and more navigable waterways than any state in the continental US. In addition, Louisville offers a metropolitan location that sits on the banks of the mighty Ohio river. Financial incentive and bountiful locations, Kentucky is the perfect location for your next film.
